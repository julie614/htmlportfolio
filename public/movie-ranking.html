Best Movies According to Julie

My top 3 movies of this past decade

Barbie

I know people are split on this pick, but I really enjoyed it and the soundtrack is great.

La La Land

Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling are great in everything! I enjoyed the music and dancing.

A Quiet Place

I was afraid to see this movie for the jump scares, but it was great and so different from other sci-fi horror films.
